## Register columns

Diplomatic transcription (as written in the source): `NUMEROS D'ORDRE, AUTEURS, DATES D'ACHAT, VENDEURS, PRIX D'ACHAT, PRIX DES REPARATIONS, PRIX DE REVIENT, DATES DES VENTES, PRIX VENDU, ACHETEURS`.

Enrichment columns (never overwrite the transcription; corrections live here):
- `INSTRUMENT_TYPE` (violin/viola), `HOUSE`, `HOUSE_MADE` (firm's own catalogue production)
NSTRUMENT_DETAIL/PRICE/PRICE_DIFF`, `DISCREPANCY_STATUS` — cross-match with E.981.8.30

## Method notes

- All prices are nominal francs; no inflation adjustment (franc depreciation 1914–26 and 1940–44 must be kept in mind for cross-period comparison).
- Two transcription typos corrected against the original images (N° 424 → 3 500 F; N° 561 → 18 000 F). Genuine source discrepancies between book price and declared export value are preserved, never averaged (N° 384, 556, 698, 709).
- Corrections welcome via pull request.
